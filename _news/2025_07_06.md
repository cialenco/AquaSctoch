At SIAM Conference on Financial Mathematics and Engeering ([FM25](https://www.siam.org/conferences-events/past-event-archive/fm25/)), July 2025, Miami, USA, Cialenco and Ludkovski organize a minisimposiym on [Strategic Sustainability: Stochastic Games for Environmental Challenges](https://meetings.siam.org/sess/dsp_programsess.cfm?SESSIONCODE=84098).   The minisymposium focused on  advances in stochastic games models  for  environmental finance applications striving to achieve dynamically sustainable management of limited natural resources.
